About Transfromapp

Transfromapp was a tool I created while taking a course on web3 and realised the need to interact with alot of unverified contracts. The tool sped up alot of my activities as interactions with unverified smart contracts became faster and easier. The tool can be used to perform the following operations.

Approval

Approve or revoke smart contract address or account address to send your various tokens

Transfer

Transfer your tokens to other address

Transfer from

Send tokens from another address after been approved to spend these tokens

Usage

ERC 20

1

  • Approval
    • Connect your wallet by clicking on the "connect wallet" button on the top right corner
    • Provide the token's contract in the first input
    • Provide the spender address (address you want to approve) in the second input
    • Provide the amout of token you want to make available for the spenders address
    • Click on the "proceed" button and approve the transaction in your wallet
  • Transfer
    • Connect your wallet by clicking on the "connect wallet" button on the top right corner
    • Provide the token's contract in the first input
    • Provide the receiver address (address you want to send the token to) in the second input
    • Provide the amout of token you want to send
    • Click on the "proceed" button and approve the transaction in your wallet
  • Transfer from
    • Connect your wallet by clicking on the "connect wallet" button on the top right corner
    • Provide the token's contract in the first input
    • Provide the from address (address you are sending token from)
    • Provide the receiver address (address you are sending the token to)
    • Provide the amout of token you want to send
    • Click on the "proceed" button and approve the transaction in your wallet

    • N.B : The wallet connected must have been approved by the "from address" and the amount approved must not exceed the amount approved.
